# Privacy Policy for CrossRoads

**Last Updated:** 11.02.2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **CrossRoads** ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

We utilize **Supabase** as our backend service provider for authentication, database, and storage. We collect information that you provide solely to us and information we collect automatically.

### A. Information You Provide to Us
*   **Account Data:** When you register, we collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials via Supabase Auth.
*   **Profile Information:** You may provide a biography, profile picture (avatar), and "Nomad" verification status.
*   **User Content:** We collect content you create, including:
    *   **Chat Messages:** Private messages sent to other users or builders.
    *   **Events:** Details of events you create or join (title, description, time).
    *   **Help Requests:** Information regarding assistance you need.
    *   **Routes & Travel Plans:** Your travel history, current location, and future travel plans (start/end points, route waypoints).

### B. Information We Collect Automatically
*   **Location Data (Critical Feature):** CrossRoads is a location-based app. We collect your precise location data to:
    *   Display your position on the map.
    *   Verify your travel routes for the "Verified Nomad" badge.
    *   Suggest nearby events, groups, and connections.
    *   Calculate travel statistics using services like OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine).
*   **Device Information:** We may collect information about your mobile device, such as device ID, model, and operating system, to ensure app compatibility and security.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
*   **Provide Core Features:** Create and manage your account, facilitate chat messaging, and display travel routes.
*   **Location Services:** Show your location relative to other nomads and events, and visualize your travel path on the map.
*   **Verification:** Verify your travel history to award "Verified" or "Pro" status badges.
*   **Social Interactions:** Enable you to join groups, RSVP to events, and connect with other travelers.
*   **Transactions:** Process "Pro" subscription purchases via RevenueCat.

## 4. Permissions We Request

To provide these features, the App may request the following permissions on your device:
*   **Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION):** Required to show your location on the map and track your travel for route verification.
*   **Camera (CAMERA):** Required to take profile photos or scan QR codes for inviting friends.
*   **Photo Library (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES):** Required to upload profile pictures or event images.
*   **Notifications:** To alert you about new messages, event updates, or help requests.

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may share information with the following third-party services that help us operate the App:
*   **Supabase:** For secure user authentication, database management, and file storage.
*   **Google Maps Platform:** To display maps and location markers.
*   **OSRM (Project-OSRM):** To calculate and render driving routes based on your travel data.
*   **RevenueCat:** TO manage in-app subscriptions and purchase validation.
*   **Expo:** For over-the-air updates and app infrastructure.

These third parties have access to your Personal Data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

*   **Public Profile:** Your name, avatar, bio, badges, and **travel routes** are visible to other users of the App to facilitate social connection.
*   **Private Chats:** Your direct messages are private between you and the recipient.
*   **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 7. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

**Your Rights:**
*   **Access & Update:** You can review and change your profile information directly within the App settings.
*   **Delete Account:** You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data. Upon deletion, your data will be removed from our Supabase database.

## 8. Security of Your Data

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

## 9. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit information from or market to children under the age of 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.
